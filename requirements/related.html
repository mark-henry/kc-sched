1.5. Related Systems

Widely used commercial systems that provide functionality comprable to the scheduler tool include:

  • aSc Timetables
  • Drools Planner
  • Lantiv Scheduling Studio
  • Mimosa Scheduling Software
  • Meeting Room Manager
  • TimeTabler
  • Visual Classroom Scheduler

Each of these systems provides a wide variety of scheduling functionality.  The goal of the KeyCow Scheduler is to provide many of the core features found in these commercially available tools, not the full range of features found in all of them.  The KeyCow Scheduler provides functional improvement in two major areas, when compared to most comercially available tools.  These improvements are:

  1. The ability to export a generated schedule in multiple formats.  In particular, the ability to export schedule information into the PeopleSoft system currently used by theoverseers in the campus scheduling department.
  2. The ability to view schedules multiple ways.  Be it an individual teacher's schedule or the schedule of a certain room's usage.
