2.4. Viewing Past Schedules

Schedule -- Current Year

When an instructor logs into his/ her account, they have the option of viewing what their schedule was in the past. This is a handy feature because it allows the instructor to keep their schedule consistent if they liked a particular quarter's schedule.


Figure 1. Instructors schedule for the current quarter (spring 2011)

Selecting Another Year

When an instructor wants to change the schedule they are viewing, they can select a quarter from the dropdown menu, indicated in figure 2. Clicking a specific quarter will change the schedule to the specified quarter.


Figure 2. Dropdown menu of past quarters. Clicking the one of interest.

Viewing Another Year

Once a user clicks a specified quarter (in this case: winter10-11) it will change from their current viewing schedule like so. The dropdown menu selector now reads the currently selected quarter.


Figure 3. The selected quarter's schedule now comes to view.
