Room Features V1

Sample Room Profile View
1. Easy to navigate side bar and top bar
2. All information is clearly shown, with features that are used often are displayed large on the screen
3. Room accessories easy to view and edit
4. Preferences button hides all user configurable items (ie. do not show room location, do not show rooms not in possession by your department, etc.)
5. (+) button adds another room, or service to the room, (-) button takes away item in room. To delete room must use preferences or options button
