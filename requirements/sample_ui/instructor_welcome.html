Interface

The instructor interface consists of a navigation bar at the top of the screen, with the currently selected option filling the rest of the page.

This is what they see when immediately after signing in:



The large icons in the center of the screen are shortcuts to the same views listed at the top of the screen.

The Preferences Tab / Shortcut opens a view that allows the professor to fill out all necessary information about the courses they want to teach, and how / when they want to teach them. All this information will be available to the scheduler and the scheduling algorithm from the administrative interface.
